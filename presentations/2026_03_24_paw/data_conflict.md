The data conflict between length comps and weight comps could be a data conflict
between certain types of fisheries, as the length comps are especially from
purse seine in equatorial waters and weight comps from longline in temperate
waters

By exploring this in more detail than in previous assessments, we may either
find a general data conflict between purse seine and longline, or a data
conflict between specific fisheries that can be examined further

Regardless of whether we can resolve data conflicts between fisheries in this
year's assessments or not, we are at least confident that we can gain some
improved understanding of the issues

Converting weights to lengths will help us diagnose the data conflict between
fisheries better, enabling us to plot size differences and trends across
different regions, etc.
