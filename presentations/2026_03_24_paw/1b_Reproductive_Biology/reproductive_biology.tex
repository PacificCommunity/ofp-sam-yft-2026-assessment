\begin{frame}{Reproductive potential}\small
  Reproductive potential is calculated as the product of three\\
  length-based processes:\\[1ex]
  \begin{enumerate}
    \item Proportion of females at length
    \item Maturity at length (of females)
    \item Fecundity at length (of mature females)
  \end{enumerate}
  \vspace{2ex}
  Sex ratio at length is calculated from Regional Observer Program\\
  data in SPC holdings\\[3ex]
  Maturity and fecundity are calculated as functions of length,\\
  using coefficients (Itano 2000)\\[2ex]
  \qquad
  $Mat_L ~=~ \left(1-(1\!-\!m) \,\times\, e^{-K(L-x)}\right)^{1/(1-m)}$
  \h{10ex}
  $Fecundity ~=~ a\times L^{\,b}$
  \vspace{2ex}
\end{frame}
